Format glossary
Round robin
Every team plays every other. Most points wins.
Swiss system
Each round paired by standings, not a fixed draw.
Knockout
Lose once and you're out. One or two legs.
Two-legged
Home and away; combined score decides the winner.
Group stage
Teams split into pools; round robin within pool.
Playoff
Separate bracket after league phase for final spots.
